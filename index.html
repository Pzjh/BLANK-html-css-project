Welcome to BLANK.

Here at BLANK. We combine techniques from all around the world to create new and interesting dishes made with ingredients from local sources and international techniques. We hope you will enjoy your time here with us for however long it might be.

Our Farm.

At our farm just 30 minutes outside Stockholm we try our best to produce ingredients that can usually not be made in the swedish climate using new technology and techniques we are proud to say we help bring swedish food production forward.

Our Ingredients.

We get 70% of our ingredients from our very own farm just outside of the city, when coming up with new dishes we always check to see what we can grow ourselves and what we can source from local vendors.
